About Mark

Mark Hetherington is a Sydney based visual artist and drummer. He has exhibited in private and artist run initiatives over the past 20 years. Primarily a painter his work is grounded in a strong foundation of drawing which he has studied in Florence under the direction of Gary Adcock. Mark has also played drums for several Sydney based bands ranging from country to rock. During the day he works as a secondary art educator and has worked at the MCA Sydney in education. His musical output can be found at:
https://taxivspigeon.bandcamp.com
https://soundcloud.com/dusty-ravens
